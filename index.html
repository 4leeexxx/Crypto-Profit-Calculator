How to Calculate Crypto Profit

The formula is straightforward: Profit = (Sell Price − Buy Price) × Amount. ROI is calculated as ((Sell Price − Buy Price) / Buy Price) × 100. For example, if you bought 1 Bitcoin at $30,000 and it's now worth $45,000, your profit is $15,000 and your ROI is 50%. Our calculator does this math instantly for any coin and any amount.

What is ROI in Crypto?

Return on Investment (ROI) is the percentage gain or loss relative to your original investment. A 100% ROI means you doubled your money. A −50% ROI means you lost half. In the volatile world of cryptocurrency, tracking ROI helps you make smarter decisions about when to hold, sell, or take profit.
